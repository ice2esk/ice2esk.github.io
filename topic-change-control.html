Change Control

Purpose: Provides a consistent, method to assess and approve modifications after requirements are met.

Change Request Lifecycle

  1. Submit change request
  2. Impact analysis
  3. Decision
  4. Implement
  5. Verify outcome and close the request

Categories (example)

  • Standard : low-risk, pre-approved
  • Normal : assessed and approved
  • Emergency : expedited with post-review

Maintains a visible change log and version history to keep teams aligned.
