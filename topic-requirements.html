Requirements Management

Definition: Requirements management is a method that enables people to trace, analyze, document, prioritize, and agree on requirements through the product development life cycle.

Types of Requirements

  • Business : organizational goals / outcomes
  • Stakeholder : needs and expectations
  • System : detailed behaviors and constraints (e.g., performance, interfaces, security)

Process

  1. Elicit & capture
  2. Analyze & clarify
  3. Define & document (SRS/PRD)
  4. Prioritize & approve
  5. Baseline & trace to design/tests
  6. Validate & verify
